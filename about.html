About Joburg Adventures

Founded in 2021, Joburg Adventures has been at the forefront of creating unforgettable African experiences for travelers from around the world. Our passion for wildlife, culture, and adventure drives us to deliver exceptional tours that go beyond the ordinary.

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we specialize in discrete, private curated adventures that showcase the best of East Africa, Europe, Asia and America. From thrilling safaris in Africa's national parks to cultural immersions, our expert guides ensure every journey is safe, educational, and truly memorable.
