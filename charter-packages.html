Charter Essentials

How Charter Works

Private chartering is simpler than you might think. Provide your travel details (route, dates, passenger count), and our team will present suitable aircraft options with transparent pricing. Hourly rates cover fuel, crew, and navigation fees. Empty-leg flights offer significant savings on pre-scheduled return trips.

Popular Routes: Nairobi-Mombasa, Nairobi-Maasai Mara (airstrips), Nairobi-Zanzibar, Nairobi-Kigali/Kampala, Nairobi-Arusha/Serengeti. We also arrange international private jet charters on request.

  • All aircraft maintained to international safety standards
  • Experienced pilots with thousands of flight hours
  • Real-time flight tracking and ground support
  • 24/7 charter coordination team
