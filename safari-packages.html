Planning Your Trip

Best Time for Safari

The Masai Mara offers year-round wildlife viewing, but the Great Migration is the highlight. From July to October, millions of wildebeest and zebra cross the Mara River in dramatic scenes. The short rains (November-December) and long rains (March-May) offer lush landscapes and fewer crowds.

Serengeti Season: The migration follows a circular pattern — catch the calving season (January-February) in the south, or river crossings (June-October) in the north.
