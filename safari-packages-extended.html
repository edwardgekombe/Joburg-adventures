Masai Mara Extended Safari

5 Days / 4 Nights | With Hot Air Balloon Experience

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
Min Guests: 2
Accommodation: Luxury Tented Camp
Highlights: Hot Air Balloon Ride

About This Safari

Deepen your wildlife experience with extra days in the reserve. Includes a magical hot air balloon ride over the Mara at sunrise, followed by a champagne breakfast in the bush. Extended game drives, visit to Maasai village, and more time to witness Great Migration.

Itinerary

D1: Nairobi - Masai Mara. Afternoon game drive. Dinner & overnight.

Pick up from Nairobi hotel at 7:00 AM. Drive through Great Rift Valley to Masai Mara. Afternoon game drive en route to your luxury tented camp. Welcome dinner and overnight.

D2: Sunrise game drive. Hot air balloon ride & champagne breakfast. Full day game drive.

Early sunrise game drive to spot predators. Return to camp for breakfast. Afternoon hot air balloon ride over the Mara with breathtaking views. Land for champagne breakfast in the bush. Return to camp for rest. Evening game drive. Dinner and overnight.

D3: Full day game drive with picnic lunch. Visit Mara River region.

Full day game drive exploring different territories. Picnic lunch in the bush. Visit Mara River area known for hippo and crocodile sightings. Return to camp for dinner and overnight.

D4: Morning game drive. Afternoon visit Maasai village. Sunset cocktails.

Morning game drive, return for breakfast. Afternoon visit to authentic Maasai village to learn about traditions, beadwork, and daily life. Evening sundowners at a scenic viewpoint. Dinner and overnight.

D5: Morning game drive, breakfast, return to Nairobi.

Final morning game drive, return to camp for breakfast. Check out and drive back to Nairobi with lunch en route. Arrive Nairobi by late afternoon.

What's Included

  • Hot air balloon ride with champagne breakfast
  • 4x4 Safari Jeep with pop-up roof
  • Professional experienced guide
  • All meals (4 breakfast, 4 lunch, 4 dinner)
  • 4 nights luxury tented camp
  • All park entrance fees
  • Game drives at sunrise & sunset
  • Maasai village visit
  • Bottled drinking water in vehicle
  • Airport/hotel transfers in Nairobi
