Planning Tips

Before Your Flight

For best visibility and photography, book morning or late afternoon flights when light is golden and wildlife is most active. Weight restrictions apply — please provide accurate passenger weights during booking. Bring sunglasses, sunscreen, and a light jacket as it's cooler at altitude.

Safety: All passengers receive a comprehensive safety briefing. Flights are weather-dependent; we may reschedule for optimal safety and visibility.

  • All flights include comprehensive insurance
  • Two-way radio communication onboard
  • Custom routes available on request
  • Complementary hotel transfers in Nairobi
