When to Go

Best Time for a Beach Holiday

Kenya's coast enjoys tropical weather year-round. The main dry seasons (December-March and July-October) offer sunny days and calm seas, perfect for beach activities and water sports. The short rains (November) and long rains (April-June) bring fewer crowds and lush, green landscapes.

Marine Life: The coral reefs are vibrant year-round. For whale shark sightings (October-March) and swimming with dolphins, the south coast around Diani is prime.

Diani Beach Resort Escape

Indulge in ultimate relaxation at Kenya's most famous beach destination. Stay at a premium beachfront resort with all-inclusive meals, water sports, and evening entertainment. Enjoy the pristine white sands, crystal-clear Indian Ocean waters, and unforgettable sunsets.

Itinerary

D1: Nairobi - Diani Beach. Transfer to resort. Check-in & beach walk.

Morning departure from Nairobi (flight or road) to Diani Beach. Transfer to your premium beachfront resort. Check-in, brief orientation, and leisurely afternoon beach walk. Welcome dinner and overnight.

D2: Full beach day. Snorkeling & water sports. Sunset dhow cruise.

Enjoy a full day at leisure with all-inclusive meals. Morning snorkeling trip to marine park (optional). Afternoon water sports (kayaking, windsurfing). Evening sunset dhow cruise with cocktails and dinner on the beach.

D3: Shimba Hills day trip. Evening entertainment.

Morning excursion to Shimba Hills National Reserve for wildlife and scenic views. Return to resort for lunch. Afternoon at leisure (spa treatments, swimming). Evening cultural show or live music at resort.

D4: Beach day. Optional deep-sea fishing. Farewell dinner.

Full day at leisure. Optional deep-sea fishing excursion (extra cost). Relax on the beach, enjoy resort amenities. Evening farewell dinner with beach BBQ or themed restaurant.

D5: Breakfast. Check-out. Transfer to Mombasa/Nairobi.

Leisurely breakfast, check-out from resort. Transfer to Mombasa airport or back to Nairobi (road/flight). Departure home or next destination.

Malindi & Watamu Beach

Explore two of Kenya's most charming coastal towns. Visit the historic Malindi town, discover the Watamu Marine National Park, and enjoy some of the best snorkeling in East Africa. This combination offers rich cultural heritage alongside pristine beaches.

Itinerary

D1: Nairobi - Mombasa - Malindi. Transfer to hotel. Evening at leisure.

Morning flight or road transfer to Mombasa, then to Malindi (approx. 2 hours). Check-in at beachfront hotel. Evening at leisure, dinner and overnight.

D2: Malindi town tour. Vasco da Gama Pillar. Afternoon at beach.

Morning guided tour of Malindi town including Vasco da Gama Pillar, old town, and local markets. Afternoon at leisure on the beach. Dinner and overnight.

D3: Transfer to Watamu. Check-in. Afternoon marine park snorkeling.

Morning transfer to Watamu (approx. 1 hour). Check-in at Watamu beach resort. Afternoon snorkeling trip in Watamu Marine National Park with guide. Dinner and overnight.

D4: Mida Creek bird watching. Local seafood cooking class.

Early morning bird watching at Mida Creek. Return for breakfast. Afternoon cooking class featuring local Swahili seafood cuisine. Evening at leisure.

D5: Free day. Optional Ruins of Gedi visit. Farewell dinner.

Full day at leisure. Optional visit to the historic Gedi Ruins (13th century). Relax on beach, enjoy water activities. Evening farewell dinner.

D6: Breakfast. Check-out. Transfer to Mombasa/Nairobi.

Final breakfast, check-out from hotel. Transfer to Mombasa airport or road back to Nairobi. Departure.

What's Included

  • 5 nights beachfront accommodation

Zanzibar Beach Extension

Extend your Kenyan adventure with a magical trip to Zanzibar. Explore the historic Stone Town (UNESCO World Heritage), relax on pristine beaches, and experience the famous Zanzibar spice culture. Perfect addition after a safari or as a standalone beach holiday.

Itinerary

D1: Nairobi - Zanzibar. Transfer to Stone Town. Check-in & explore.

Morning flight from Nairobi to Zanzibar (approx. 2 hours). Transfer to Stone Town hotel. Check-in, brief orientation. Afternoon walking tour of Stone Town's narrow streets, markets, and architecture. Dinner and overnight.

D2: Stone Town full day tour. Palace Museum, House of Wonders.

Full day exploring Stone Town's UNESCO sites: Sultan's Palace Museum, House of Wonders, Old Fort, and Anglican Cathedral. Lunch at a local restaurant. Evening at leisure.

D3: Spice plantation tour. Afternoon transfer to beach resort.

Morning visit to a spice plantation to see cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon, and other spices. Learn about Zanzibar's spice trade history. Afternoon transfer to beach resort on the east coast. Check-in and relax.

D4: Beach day. Optional dhow cruise. Swimming with dolphins.

Full day at leisure on the beach. Optional traditional dhow cruise at sunset. Optional dolphin watching tour (highly recommended). Enjoy resort amenities, swimming, snorkeling.

D5: Prison Island excursion. Seafood dinner on the beach.

Morning boat trip to Prison Island (Changu) to see giant Aldabra tortoises. Afternoon at leisure. Evening seafood dinner on the beach with Swahili cuisine.

D6: Breakfast. Check-out. Transfer to Zanzibar airport. Fly to Nairobi.

Final breakfast, check-out from resort. Transfer to Zanzibar airport for flight back to Nairobi. Arrival in Nairobi by late morning.

What's Included

  • 2 nights Stone Town + 3 nights beach resort

  • Dolphin watching (optional)
